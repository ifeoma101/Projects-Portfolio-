About Me

I bring a unique blend of healthcare expertise and a keen interest in front-end web development to the table. With a strong foundation in pharmacy and a burgeoning proficiency in web development, I am excited to leverage my diverse background to create innovative solutions that seamlessly blend healthcare and technology.
